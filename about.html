About Us

We Are India’s Trusted
Height Safety Experts

Horizzon Safety Solution Pvt. Ltd. delivers innovative, certified height safety solutions for industries where safety at height is critical. Our mission is to protect lives by designing, manufacturing, and installing robust fall protection systems for oil & gas, steel, railways, metros, and high-rise buildings. We combine advanced engineering with industry expertise to ensure every project meets the highest standards of safety and compliance.
