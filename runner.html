HS107 Runner: Spark-Free Mobility for Horizontal Lifelines

At Horizzon Safety Solution Pvt. Ltd., we prioritize both uncompromised safety and operational efficiency in elevated work environments. Our HS107 Runner is a cutting-edge component of horizontal lifeline systems, uniquely engineered to provide seamless and spark-free movement for workers. Designed to glide effortlessly along SS wire ropes, this runner ensures continuous fall protection, making it an ideal solution for environments where minimizing ignition sources is critical, such as in potentially explosive atmospheres.

The core innovation of the HS107 Runner lies in its construction from SS-316 material (Stainless Steel). This specific grade of stainless steel is chosen not only for its superior strength and corrosion resistance but critically, for its properties that ensure no spark, friction, or static electricity is generated as it moves along the SS wire rope. This feature makes the HS107 an indispensable safety device in sensitive industrial settings where such hazards must be strictly mitigated. Furthermore, its intelligent design allows the runner to pass easily through intermediate brackets and corner brackets without any disconnection to the device, guaranteeing uninterrupted safety and smooth traversal of complex lifeline layouts.

The HS107 Runner is designed for straightforward and secure operation, enhancing user safety and productivity. To use, simply insert the wire rope into the runner, align it, and match the bottom 12mm hole with your karabiner. Once the karabiner is inserted into the 12mm hole and locked, the worker is securely attached and ready to move along the lifeline. For removal, the process is equally simple: unlock the karabiner, take it outside the 12mm hole, and remove the runner from the wire rope. This intuitive design ensures quick and safe attachment and detachment, reflecting Horizzon Safety Solution's commitment to practical and advanced fall protection.
