HS108 U-BOLT CLAMP: Secure Your Lifeline - Uninterrupted Fall Protection for Industrial Buildings

Ensure robust safety for your roof workers with the HS108 U-BOLT CLAMP, a critical component of our comprehensive Fall Protection System for Industrial Buildings.

Designed and manufactured to stringent quality standards and EN norms, the HS108 U-BOLT CLAMP provides an unwavering grip for wire ropes, effectively eliminating the danger of loose wires. Crafted from high-grade SS316 and SS304 stainless steel, these clamps are built to withstand various temperatures and climates, ensuring reliable performance in diverse industrial environments.

As a vital part of a lifeline system that allows uninterrupted movement across the roof, our U-Bolt Clamps are strategically employed at both ends of the system, with two pieces utilized at each end for maximum security. This meticulous design reflects our commitment to providing adequate safety against the hazards of falls from height.

We are a leading manufacturer, supplier, installer, and service provider of fall protection systems, offering solutions tailored to specific project requirements and customer budgets. The HS108 U-BOLT CLAMP embodies our dedication to easy-to-use and understand products that deliver superior fall protection.
