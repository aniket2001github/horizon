HS110 STAINLESS STEEL EYE BOLT: The Critical Anchor for Uncompromised Fall Protection

The HS110 Stainless Steel Eye Bolt serves as a foundational anchor point, providing unwavering strength and reliability for the critical lifeline in your industrial fall protection system.

Designed to withstand extreme forces, this robust eye bolt is crafted from high-quality Stainless Steel 304, ensuring superior corrosion resistance and long-term durability in diverse industrial environments. Its primary role is to secure the lifeline firmly to the end anchor posts, forming the backbone of a system that guarantees the safety of workers operating at height.

With a breaking strength exceeding 40kN (kilonewtons), the HS110 Eye Bolt provides a significant safety margin, far surpassing the typical forces encountered during fall arrest scenarios. This exceptional strength ensures that the eye bolt can reliably hold the full lifeline, even under dynamic loading conditions. Integrated seamlessly into our comprehensive fall protection solutions, the HS110 Stainless Steel Eye Bolt is a testament to our commitment to engineering products that prioritize safety, performance, and peace of mind.

HS110 STAINLESS STEEL EYE BOLT: Detailed Specifications

Detailed specifications for the HS110 Stainless Steel Eye Bolt, a critical component in fall protection systems.

Feature Specification
Product Name HS110 STAINLESS STEEL EYE BOLT
Material Composition Stainless Steel 304 (SS304): Chosen for its excellent resistance to corrosion, rust, and various chemical agents. SS304 provides a durable and long-lasting anchor solution suitable for harsh outdoor and industrial environments, ensuring the structural integrity of the fall protection system over time.
Primary Function
  • End Anchor Post Connection: Serves as the primary attachment point for the lifeline to the end anchor posts. It securely links the wire rope lifeline to the fixed structural elements of the building, forming a crucial part of the fall protection barrier.
  • Lifeline Support: Designed to hold and bear the full load of the lifeline, ensuring its stability and operational readiness in the event of a fall.
Breaking Strength More than 40kN (Kilonewtons): This exceptional breaking strength indicates a high capacity to withstand tensile forces before fracture. A breaking strength exceeding 40kN provides a substantial safety factor, significantly higher than the forces typically generated during a fall arrest, ensuring reliable performance and worker safety even under extreme conditions.
Application
  • Both End Anchor Posts: Utilized at the critical termination points of the fall protection lifeline system. These are the points where the lifeline is anchored to the building structure, making the strength and reliability of the eye bolt paramount.
  • Industrial Buildings: Specifically designed for integration into fall protection systems on industrial structures, roofs, and elevated platforms where workers are exposed to fall hazards.
Safety Objective
  • Critical Component for Fall Arrest: Ensures the lifeline remains securely tensioned and anchored, providing the necessary strength to absorb and dissipate the energy of a fall, thereby preventing a worker from hitting the ground.
  • Worker Safety: Directly contributes to compliance with safety regulations and the overall well-being of personnel working at height by providing a reliable and robust anchor point.
