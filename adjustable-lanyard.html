HS114 Adjustable Lanyard: Versatile and Secure Positioning

The HS114 Adjustable Lanyard from Horizzon Safety Solution Pvt. Ltd. offers a robust and flexible solution for work positioning and restraint, providing operators with the freedom of movement necessary for their tasks while ensuring uncompromised safety. This high-quality lanyard is an essential component of any fall protection system, designed to adapt to various working heights and environments.

At the core of the HS114 lanyard is its durable Ø 12mm Polyamide rope. Polyamide, known for its high tensile strength and resistance to abrasion, ensures longevity and reliability in demanding conditions. What truly sets this lanyard apart is its impressive length options, available in 10-meter, 15-meter, 20-meter, and other customized lengths. This versatility gives operators the required operational flexibility, allowing them to adjust their working radius precisely and efficiently without needing to frequently re-anchor.

Safety and durability are further reinforced by the grab metal assembly, which is meticulously crafted from Zinc-plated yellow passivation Steel Alloy (5mm sheet). This robust construction material provides exceptional strength and corrosion resistance, ensuring the assembly's integrity even in harsh outdoor conditions. Crucially, the metal assembly is designed to be free of sharp edges, minimizing the risk of damage to the rope, other equipment, or the user's hands during operation. The HS114 Adjustable Lanyard is a testament to Horizzon Safety Solution's commitment to providing safe, reliable, and user-friendly equipment for work at height.
